The AI verification infrastructure that turns manual transfers (CliQ, InstaPay, ZainCash) into automated checkout.

⚠ The Problem

  • Card adoption is dangerously low.
    80% of MENA customers don't use cards online. Merchants relying on cards lose the majority of the market.
  • Wallets are manual and disconnected.
    CliQ and InstaPay are P2P only. Merchants force users to take "Screenshots" of payments. It's unscalable.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) kills margins.
    High return rates and delayed settlement for merchants who accept cash.
